The American Dream (2018)

Documentary, HD Video, (In Production). Virender Rana, once an undocumented migrant worker from India, chases his dream of becoming a filmmaker while working as a taxi driver in America. Shot over a decade, an intimate portrait of an immigrant following his dreams.
